gridXchange

click here to view the project

This project was a prototype of an innovative energy trading app designed to facilitate the sale of excess energy produced by individuals or businesses to municipalities and other high-demand electricity consumers. This app streamlines the process of settling municipal energy bills, creating a sustainable ecosystem. Users can easily offer surplus energy to the grid, reducing their costs and contributing to a cleaner energy mix. The platform ensures efficient energy distribution and enables a win-win situation for both suppliers and energy consumers, fostering sustainability and cost-effectiveness in the electricity sector.
